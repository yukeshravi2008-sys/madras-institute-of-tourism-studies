About MITS - Tourism Education Hub

MITS acts as an interface between the tourism industry and academics. It provides awareness about the need for tourism education, skill development, and sensitization of employment and entrepreneurial opportunities.

Our Vision

MITS has been established with a vision to provide high-quality education in the field of tourism and hospitality, training young professionals to be the face of the future tourism industry.

Highly Skilled Trainers

We provide trainers from all over India, making MITS a one-stop destination for tourism and hospitality-related education.

Bridging the Gap

We provide proper training to our students so they can be placed in reputed organizations and grow as professionals.

Research & Consultancy

We create awareness regarding the tourism sector and offer research and consultancy for institutes and industries.
